About

My name is Evan Taylor, a current front-end developer with extensive experience in CMS, UX & UI design, and the overall production behind a website. My background in web development aids in my creative and analytical flair. I consider myself a polymath, constantly improving my academic and practical knowledge in technological advances and developments.

My recent exposure to data science has piqued my interests, however I am adamant on mastering front-end web development prior to exploring data science any further. Wordpress has improved my efficiency and speed, but I find that the level of abstraction has made it too simple. Going forward, I aim to improve my front-end skills by using bespoke JS frameworks, and hopefully Python.

As someone who has no tertiary background in pure mathematics and computer science, I faced some challenges throughout my data science course. Learning the mathematical processes used in machine learning had pushed me to my mental limits. However, these challenges confirmed one thing, if I am able to grasp such knowledge without the necessary background, I am more than capable of learning and mastering anything I put my mind to. I learn by doing, as they say, ‘practice makes perfect’. I look forward to future learning experiences in front-end and software development.
